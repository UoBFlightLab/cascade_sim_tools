# Getting Started with Ardupilot Software-in-the-Loop Simulator

## Through Mission Planner

Install Mission Planner and launch it

Move to mission Flight Plan screen and use Load WP File button to load bridge_low.waypoints
Click 'Yes' to reset home - you should now see a mission

Move to Simulation screen and click Multirotor
(might need to approve Firewall access at this point)
should bounce you over to the Flight Data screen
Check green 'connected' plugs at top right and use status tab bottom left to see numbers changing
Should see a little quadrotor icon over middle of the bridge

Pop back to Flight Plan and click 'Write WPs'
Then go back to Flight Data and should see the mission

Move to actions tab at bottom left.
In quick succession: click arm, right click map, choose take-off, and enter 25 as altitude
Artificial horizon at top left should wobble and show climb to altitude

In actions tab, click Auto.
Should see drone follow mission - speed 3m/s

### Companion computers

On-board: TCP port 5762 or 5763 to talk straight to drone (sim)

Ground: in Mission Planner, press Ctrl+F, click Mavlink, choose 'TCP Host - 14550' and enable 'Write access'.  Click start.  Change port if you like.  Then use this port to talk to the drone through Mission Planner.
